WEBRip Quality:

Movies marked with "WEB" in the title are in WEBRip quality. WEBRips are captured from streaming services like Netflix, Amazon Prime, etc. While offering good quality and convenience, they may not match Blu-ray's superior video and audio clarity.

Blu-ray Quality:

Movies without additional labels are in Blu-ray quality. Blu-ray discs offer exceptional video and audio fidelity, providing the ultimate home entertainment experience. With Blu-ray, you'll enjoy crystal-clear visuals, immersive sound, and HD details that truly bring movies to life.
